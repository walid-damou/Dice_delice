Cancellation Policy

We understand that plan changes may occur and we are happy to cancel your reservation if necessary. However, we ask that you notify us as soon as possible if you need to cancel. If you cancel your reservation at least 24 hours before the reservation time, no fee will be charged. If you cancel less than 24 hours before the reservation time, we reserve the right to charge a cancellation fee equivalent to 50% of the cost of the reservation. If you do not show up for your reservation without prior cancellation, we will charge a cancellation fee equivalent to 100% of the cost of the reservation.
